## 2. 功能需求

### FUNC-001: BSW 初始化
- The system SHALL initialize all BSW layers in the correct AUTOSAR order
- The system SHALL verify each module's initialization status

### FUNC-002: CAN 通信
- The system SHALL support CAN 2.0 and CAN FD at 500kbps
- The system SHALL provide DCM UDS diagnostic services over CAN

### FUNC-003: 诊断功能
- The system SHALL implement UDS (ISO 14229) diagnostic services
- The system SHALL support DTC storage and retrieval (ISO 15031-6)

### FUNC-004: NVRAM 管理
- The system SHALL provide persistent parameter storage via NvM+Fee
- The system SHALL support block-level CRC verification

### FUNC-005: 调度管理
- The system SHALL provide 10ms/100ms/1000ms scheduling base cycles
- The system SHALL support AUTOSAR schedule manager (SchM)

## 3. 验收场景

### Scenario: 正常启动
- GIVEN the ECU is powered on
- WHEN the BSW initialization sequence completes
- THEN the main scheduling loop SHALL be running
- AND CAN communication SHALL be operational

### Scenario: UDS 诊断会话
- GIVEN the CAN bus is operational
- WHEN a tester sends DiagnosticSessionControl (0x10 0x03)
- THEN the ECU SHALL respond with 0x50 0x03

### Scenario: DTC 记录
- GIVEN a monitored signal exceeds its valid range
- WHEN the SW-C reports the fault via Dem_ReportErrorStatus
- THEN the DTC SHALL be stored in NvM with FAILED status

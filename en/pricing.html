FAQ

Pricing FAQ

Are there any feature limitations on the Free plan?
The Free plan is limited to 1 project with a basic AI Agent pipeline (no full SDD→DDD→TDD pipeline) and Layer 1 CI/CD only. It's perfect for personal evaluation and open source project trials.
Can I get a refund for the Pro plan?
Monthly subscribers are eligible for a 7-day no-questions-asked refund. Annual subscribers may receive a full refund within the first month. For refund requests, please email our support team.
Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?
Absolutely. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated pricing. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.
Does the Enterprise plan support private deployment?
Yes. The Enterprise plan supports fully private deployment with Helm Chart for Kubernetes, SSO/OAuth identity provider integration, and a dedicated customer success manager.

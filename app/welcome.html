🎉 Welcome to yuleOSH!

yuleOSH is your open-source specification-driven development platform. It's designed to bridge the gap between requirements and implementation, ensuring every line of code is traceable back to a spec.

  • Spec-First Development — Write specs, not tickets
  • Automated Pipeline — From spec to CI, all in one flow
  • Traceability — Every requirement mapped to evidence
  • Multi-tenant — Organizations, projects, and teams
  • Open Source — MIT licensed, built for everyone

## 1. System Requirements

## 4. Services Layer

### SVC-001: Com (Communication Stack)
- The Com module SHALL route signal-based PDUs between SW-Cs and bus interfaces
- The Com SHALL support signal groups, IPDUs, and PDU groups
- The Com SHALL provide timing and notification callbacks

### SVC-007: SchM (Schedule Manager)

### SVC-008: PduR (PDU Router)

### SVC-009: E2E (End-to-End Communication Protection)
- The E2E SHALL provide CRC-based protection for safety-critical signals
- The E2E SHALL support profile 1 (NRC) and profile 2 (CRC)

### SVC-010: Det (Default Error Tracer)

## 7. Test Plan

### Scenario: MCU Initialization
- GIVEN the system is powered on
- WHEN Mcu_Init() is called with the configuration
- THEN the PLL SHALL lock at 120MHz
- AND the system tick timer SHALL be configured

### Scenario: DIO Channel Write
- GIVEN the DIO driver is initialized
- WHEN Dio_WriteChannel() is called with STD_HIGH
- THEN the target GPIO pin SHALL output a high level

### Scenario: CAN Message TX/RX
- GIVEN the CAN driver is initialized and configured to 500kbps
- WHEN Can_Write() transmits a CAN frame
- THEN the CAN hardware SHALL send the frame to the bus
- AND the TX confirmation callback SHALL be invoked

### Scenario: UDS Diagnostic Session
- GIVEN DCM is initialized and CAN is operational
- WHEN a tester sends DiagnosticSessionControl (0x10) with sub-function 0x03 (extended session)
- THEN the ECU SHALL respond with a positive response (0x50 0x03)

### Scenario: DTC Storage
- GIVEN Dem is initialized
- WHEN an event with severity ERROR is reported via Dem_ReportErrorStatus()
- THEN the DTC record SHALL be stored with confirmed status
- AND the fault status SHALL persist across power cycles

### Scenario: NvM Block Write
- GIVEN NvM is initialized with Fee/Flash backing store
- WHEN NvM_WriteBlock() is called for a configured block
- THEN the block data SHALL be written to non-volatile memory
- AND a subsequent read-back SHALL match the written data
